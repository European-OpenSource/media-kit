## About Us

### Description

European OpenSource is a community-driven platform connecting developers, companies, and institutions across Europe to build a transparent and sovereign digital future through open-source innovation. We provide a comprehensive catalog of European open-source projects, fostering collaboration and strengthening digital independence.

### Mission

Building a platform to unite the European open-source ecosystem by connecting those who build, fund, and adopt open technology. We work for a free and independent Europe.

### Vision

We envision a world where technology serves as a means, grounded in freedom, to help build a more just and equitable society
